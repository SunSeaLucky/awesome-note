## 制作一个能够匀速上下移动的正方体

```python
import bpy

bpy.ops.mesh.primitive_cube_add()

cube = bpy.context.active_object
cube.name='MyCube'

cube.keyframe_insert('location', frame=1)

cube.location.z = 5

cube.keyframe_insert('location' , frame=90)

cube.location.z = 0

cube.keyframe_insert('location' , frame=180)
```
